Chapter 1: An UnQuiet Beach

Dirk Dainsworth lay sprawled on the beach chair, his powerful frame a study in stillness beneath the last, bleeding rays of the sun. The green of his swimming trunks was a muted contrast to the vibrant, dying day. He was, to all appearances, a man at peace, soaking up the warmth, letting the ocean's breath wash over him.
